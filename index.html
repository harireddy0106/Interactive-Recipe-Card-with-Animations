Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins | Serving Size: 8

Price: ₹350.00

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a cake pan.
  3. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  4. Mix wet ingredients in another bowl.
  5. Combine both mixtures.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  8. Let the cake cool before serving.
